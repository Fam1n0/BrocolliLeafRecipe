Stir-fried Broccoli Leaves with Garlic and Chili Flakes
Ingredients:
  • Broccoli leaves from one bunch, washed and chopped
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 red chili, deseeded and finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • A splash of lemon juice or a sprinkle of zest (optional)
  • 2 teaspoons of soy sauce (optional)
Instructions:
  1. Heat a large pan or wok over medium heat. Add the olive oil.
  2. Add the minced garlic and chili to the oil and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add the chopped broccoli leaves to the pan.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Add a splash of lemon juice or zest for a fresh flavor.
  6. Serve hot as a side dish.
